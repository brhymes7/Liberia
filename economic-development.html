Economic Development

Building sustainable livelihoods through agricultural training, microfinance, and small business development programs.

Our Vision

Economic empowerment is key to breaking the cycle of poverty and creating lasting change. Our economic development program focuses on building local capacity, creating sustainable income opportunities, and fostering entrepreneurship within communities.

We believe in empowering individuals and communities with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to create their own economic opportunities and build prosperous futures.
