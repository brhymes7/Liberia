About Change the Narrative

Founded in the heart of Liberia, a nonprofit organization committed to transforming global perceptions of Liberia while directly supporting communities through education, food programs, and essential resources.

Our Mission

To educate, uplift, and empower Liberians while reshaping how the world understands our nation.

Community-Centered Approach

Working directly with local leaders and communities

Sustainable Development

Long-term solutions that create lasting impact

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives addressing critical community needs

Education

Providing quality education resources, teacher training, and literacy programs to ensure every child has access to learning opportunities.

  • • School infrastructure development
  • • Teacher capacity building
  • • Adult literacy programs
  • • Educational material distribution

Food and Health Relief

Providing essential food assistance and health relief services to communities in need through direct aid and sustainable programs.

  • • Emergency food distribution
  • • Nutritional support programs
  • • Health relief services
  • • Community health initiatives

Economic Development

Supporting sustainable livelihoods through agricultural training, microfinance, and small business development programs.

  • • Agricultural training & tools
  • • Microfinance support
  • • Business development
  • • Market access facilitation
