Our Story

I first traveled to Liberia in 2021 during a time when I felt lost and uncertain about my purpose. What I found was more than a country â€” I found clarity, connection, and a calling.

Though I was born in the United States, Liberia has always been part of me. I was conceived there, and my mother fled during the civil war while pregnant, sacrificing everything so I could be born into safety while still rooted in our culture.

Returning to the motherland changed me.

Each visit strengthened my love for my people and deepened my desire to serve. I witnessed hardship, but I also witnessed strength, joy, brilliance, and untapped potential.

Change The Narrative: Liberia was born from that awakening.
